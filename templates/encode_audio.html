Your voice recording is compressed (50-70% size reduction) and embedded into the image pixels using LSB steganography. The image looks identical but contains your hidden audio.

Storage Capacity:

  • 1920x1080 (Full HD): ~0.74 MB storage
  • 3840x2160 (4K): ~3 MB storage
  • 7680x4320 (8K): ~12 MB storage
  • For longer recordings, use very large images or multiple images
  • Max upload size: 1000MB (1GB)

Tip: For long recordings, use the included create_large_image.py script to generate large blank images with sufficient storage capacity.
